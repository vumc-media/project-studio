About

Original music shaped around the story, purpose, and sound behind the idea.

Track Lab is a composition and production brand for artists, writers, filmmakers, churches, and content creators.

Whether you're starting with lyrics, a melody, a rough recording, a visual scene, or simply a musical idea, the goal is to help shape it into something clear, useful, and emotionally connected.

Process

How Projects Begin

Every custom project starts with a conversation about sound, story, direction, and purpose.

1

Share the Idea

Send a project request with where you are in the process and the direction you have in mind.

2

Talk Through the Sound

We’ll discuss genre, mood, references, story, purpose, and what you want the finished piece to feel like.

3

Build the Production

The project is developed through composition, arranging, editing, production, and collaboration.
