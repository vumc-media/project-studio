Process

Every Composition Begins with a Story

Whether developing a new cue or preparing music for publisher review, the process begins with emotion, purpose, and the story being told.

1

Discover the Story

We identify the mood, purpose, audience, pacing, references, and emotional direction behind the project.

2

Compose the Emotion

The cue is shaped through melody, harmony, rhythm, texture, and atmosphere so it supports the visual moment.

3

Deliver the Sound

Final music is prepared for the intended use, whether for TAXI submission, publisher review, media production, or custom delivery.
